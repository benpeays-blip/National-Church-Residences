1. Raiser’s Edge NXT (CRM)
Core donor database: Holds donor records, pledges, tributes, planned giving.
Strengths: Trusted by boards, integrates with WealthEngine, stable for major gifts.
Weaknesses: Poor reporting, clunky UX, limited AI, weak adoption by frontline fundraisers.
Role at NCR: The “system of record” for donor relationships, but needs augmentation for analytics and usability.

2. DonorSearch
Prospecting engine: Surfaces philanthropic giving, faith-based donors, foundation ties.
Strengths: Excellent charitable giving data, strong affinity scoring, good for batch screenings.
Weaknesses: False positives, clunky UI, not embedded in RE workflows, underused without training.
Role at NCR: Could complement WealthEngine by identifying hidden donors, but adoption requires SOPs and integration.

3. Power BI
Analytics layer: Connects RE NXT, Yardi, Workday, PointClickCare, finance systems.
Strengths: Real-time dashboards, customizable visualizations, integrates across Microsoft ecosystem.
Weaknesses: Requires clean data and technical skill.
Role at NCR: Bridges the reporting gap in RE NXT, giving executives donor pipeline, occupancy, and grant dashboards.

4. Instrumentl
Grant discovery tool: Finds philanthropic and local grants, tracks deadlines, organizes pipeline.
Strengths: Best for foundation grants, clean UI, automated reminders, affordable.
Weaknesses: Not built for federal/HUD compliance, weak CRM integrations.
Role at NCR: Excellent supplement for foundation and local grants, but not sufficient for HUD/state compliance-heavy grants.

5. Workday
HR + payroll backbone: Manages employees, pay, recruiting, compliance.
Strengths: Secure, enterprise-class, strong HR workflows.
Weaknesses: Overly complex, poor frontline adoption, hard to customize.
Role at NCR: System of record for staff, but needs simplification and integration with housing/clinical systems.

6. PointClickCare
Clinical EHR: Resident health records, care coordination, compliance in senior living.
Role at NCR: Core for healthcare operations, must integrate with housing, finance, and HR systems.

7. GuideStar / Candid
Transparency + funder intelligence: Nonprofit profiles, 990s, foundation giving history.
Strengths: Trusted by donors, definitive source for legitimacy.
Weaknesses: Outdated data, poor integrations, expensive for deep use.
Role at NCR: Due diligence and reputation management; complements Instrumentl for grant prospecting.

8. SignUpGenius
Volunteer coordination tool: Simple sign-ups for events, shifts, church activities.
Role at NCR: Useful for community engagement, volunteer scheduling, small-group coordination.
