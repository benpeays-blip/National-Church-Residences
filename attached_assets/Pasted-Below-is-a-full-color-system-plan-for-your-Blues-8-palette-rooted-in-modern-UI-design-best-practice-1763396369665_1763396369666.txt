2. Design Principles

Contrast and readability

Always keep text at least WCAG AA compliant: dark blues (b6–b8) for light backgrounds; white text on b6–b8 when used as fills.

Avoid using b1–b3 behind dark text and dark buttons; too little contrast.

Depth hierarchy

Use darker shades to indicate elevation or importance.

Example: Header (b8) → Section header (b6) → Card header (b5) → Body background (b2–b3).

Consistency

Each layer (page, section, card, component) should have a predictable tone shift — about 1–2 shades difference max.

Accent discipline

Keep one true accent blue (often b6 or b7) for all CTA buttons and links.

Use neutral grays (not other blues) for text and borders to avoid “washed-out” effect.
